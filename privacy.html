Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy outlines how your personal information is handled by the Share Status mobile application ("the App"). 1. Information We Do Not Collect The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information about its users. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal data. 2. Information and Permissions Collected by Your Device To function correctly, the App requires access to certain features on your device. These permissions are requested from you and are used solely to provide the App's core functionality. Media Library / Photos: We request permission to access your device's photo and media library. This is to allow you to select images to use in your creations and to save your final designs directly to your gallery. We do not access or collect any of your photos without your explicit action. Local Storage: The App uses local storage on your device (via AsyncStorage) to save your favorited templates. This data is stored locally on your device and is not accessed or transmitted by us. 3. Third-Party Services The App uses third-party services that may collect information. The privacy policies of these third-party services govern their use of your information. Firebase: We use the Firebase Realtime Database, a service provided by Google, to fetch and display the app's templates and categories. We do not use Firebase to store any user-specific or personalized data. You can read Google's privacy policy for Firebase here. AdMob: The App may display banner ads provided by Google's AdMob service. AdMob may collect and use certain information to show relevant ads. You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites and apps that use their services here. 4. How We Use Your Information Any information accessed by the App is used for the sole purpose of enabling the App's features: To allow you to add your own photos to templates. To save your edited designs to your device's photo gallery. To enable direct sharing of your creations to other apps. To provide you with a list of available templates and categories from our database. 5. Data Security We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App is designed to minimize the collection of personal data, and any data accessed is handled on your local device. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. 7. Contact Us If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
